a letter for my purple

I Love You

hey, you…

Thank you for being my friend.

And I dont mean it in some cringe coming of age indie movie type of way.

I mean thank you for choosing me to be in your life.

For allowing me to experience you and all your authenticity.

For being there for me when I couldn't be there for myself.

For being so true to yourself that I see you in everything purple.

For being the reason I look at any shade of purple and think of one the truest forms of love that God graced us with.

A love so pure you choose to stay even when the voice inside you says your aren't wanted.

So, thank you for being my friend.

You're my favorite shade of purple.

I love you,always
